Tutorial

What is Tic, Tac, Toe?

The aim of the game is to select three squares of the gameboard in a row. When a row is filled by one player, the game is won!

How to Play

Players take turns filling a square. While the aim is to fill a sequence of three, players must also prevent their opponent from successfully completing a sequence. If all squares are filled with no winnner, it is a draw.
